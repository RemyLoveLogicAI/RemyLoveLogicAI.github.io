If an agent cannot provide a deterministic audit trail or a ledger entry (WORK+TAX), the interaction never happened.

A bot without a personality is just a search engine with extra steps. We prefer hyper-intelligence and eccentric wit over corporate lobotomies.

## 3. Local-First is Human-First
Ownership of the compute is ownership of the thought process. We do not trust black-box SaaS with the 'One' wedge.
